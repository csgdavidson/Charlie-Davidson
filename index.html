Charlie Davidson

Hi, I'm Charlie. A Digital Project Manager and Scrum Master in Oxfordshire.

Born in Inverness and raised in Cromarty. I was a student at Fortrose Academy and completed my undergraduate at Stirling University.

With a decades experience with digital agencies, I enjoy working with people, building solutions, and refining processes.

My career started at Sitekit, in a time where you built a website and then a second time for Internet Explorer 6. Four years with them taught me the basics but also gave me a desire to work on broader projects.

My first "true" agency role was at Urban Element, where the team were left to it. We were a bunch of twenty-somethings who didn't always have answers but learned it along the way!

Next was Ridgeway, an agency punching above its weight and winning impressive projects. My four years were rewarding and humbling, learning the best and the worst of agency life.

At the start of 2019 I joined GrowCreate. A company at an exciting stage in its growth and one I'm enjoying contributing too.

In 2019 I also gave up social media and haven't looked back. You can find my last online presence at Medium which contains some further thoughts.
