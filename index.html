Charlie Davidson

Hi, I'm Charlie. A Digital Project Manager and Scrum Master in Oxfordshire.

Born in Inverness and raised in Cromarty, I went to Fortrose Academy and completed my undergraduate at Stirling University. I've worked with digital agencies for eleven years and enjoy working with people, building solutions, and refining processes.

My digital career started at Sitekit. In 2009 you built a website and then again for IE6. I learnt the basics but also desired to work on broader projects.

I joined Urban Elementto get that. We were a bunch of twenty-somethings who didn't always have answers but learned along the way!

Next was Ridgeway, an agency with impressive projects. My four years were rewarding and humbling, learning the best and worst of agency life.

At the start of 2019, I joined GrowCreate and have had a blast since. I expect to see exciting things from them going forward.

I also gave up social media in 2019 and haven't looked back. You can find my last online presence at Medium though.
